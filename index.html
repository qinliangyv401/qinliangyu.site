Liangyu Qin is a Senior Graphic Designer and Art Director with cutting-edge design insights and an international aesthetic.His professional experience includes working on creative projects for global brands and deeply engaging in Visual Direction for fashion Brand Runway Shows and the Visual System Development for Fashion Concept Store.These experiences have equipped him with a strong ability to decode brand DNA and execute exceptional creative solutions.

He is proficient in Adobe Creative Suite and motion design tools, with particular expertise in using After Effects for dynamic design, developing visual assets for projects.Additionally, his extensive experience in photography and art direction enables him to collaborate effectively with creative teams, overseeing the entire process from concept development to final output.

As a designer who consistently maintains a sharp insight into avant-garde aesthetics and trend culture, he has the ability to infuse brands with groundbreaking creative perspectives, creating brand assets that combine aesthetic excellence with market influence.
